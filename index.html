ⓘ How the SMS score is calculated
Click to expand ▾

Every client in your database is scored out of 100 points across four signals, with a penalty for no-shows. The top 500 by score are shown below. Clients who already have a future booking are automatically excluded. Clients who visited very recently score lower on purpose.

R
Recency max 40 pts

How long ago the client last visited. The sweet spot is 30–180 days.

Just visited (≤30 days)10 pts
30–90 days40 pts
90–180 days30 pts
180 days – 1 year15 pts
Over 1 year5 pts
O
Overdue Bonus max 20 pts

Based on the client's personal visit rhythm.

Formula: (days overdue ÷ average gap) × 20, capped at 20 pts.

F
Frequency max 20 pts

Visits per year, rewarding loyal regulars.

Formula: (visits ÷ years as client) × 3, capped at 20 pts.

M
Spend Value max 20 pts

Average spend per visit.

Formula: avg spend ÷ 5, capped at 20 pts.

!
No-show Penalty up to −15 pts

Each recorded no-show deducts 3 points (capped at −15).
